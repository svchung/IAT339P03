About Me

I am an Interactive Arts and Technology student at Simon Fraser University pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Arts and Technology.


By nature, I am a genuinely curious person which has not only been a trademark personality trait but it allows me to create intentional and aesthetic designs that provide seamless user interaction.


I am currently seeking to start my career as a product designer where I can create technological interfaces using simplistic solutions that provide memorable influences.


To me what creates these memorable influences are simple yet beautiful designs but ones that provide a harmonious interactive experience for users. Through mediums such as Figma, ProCreate and Adobe Illustrator, I am able to create meaningful interfaces that draw inspiration from memorable designs to even places I have encountered.
